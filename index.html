Young Romantics

Alternative Rock from Bowling Green, KY.

Young Romantics formed in Bowling Green, KY, in early 2017 when high school friends Matt Porter and Griffin Fletcher started making music while studying at Western Kentucky University.

The duo began by recording in Porter’s Honda Accord, releasing two self-produced EPs to SoundCloud between November 2017 and January 2018.

With the addition of Mitchell Manuel and Gerek Patrick in late 2018, the band recorded its first full-band EP, In Case You Feel the Same, which released August 30, 2019.

Consisting of Fletcher (vocals), Porter (drums), Manuel (guitar) and Gabriel Rogers (bass), the band is currently recording its sophomore project, In Case You Don’t, a seven-song EP anticipated for release in 2021.
